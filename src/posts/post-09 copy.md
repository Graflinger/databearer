# Web Accessibility Fundamentals

Building accessible websites ensures everyone can use and enjoy your content.

## Key Principles

- Semantic HTML
- Keyboard navigation
- ARIA labels
- Color contrast

Accessibility isn't just good practice—it's the right thing to do for all users.
